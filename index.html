Intermediate

Scholarships for intermediate (12th grade) students can be limited compared to scholarships available for undergraduate or postgraduate studies. However, there are still some scholarship opportunities for students at this level such as Merit-Based Scholarships.

Degree

Scholarships for undergraduate (UG) students are widely available, and they come from various sources, including governments, universities, private organizations, and foundations. The availability of scholarships can vary by country and region

Post Graduation

Scholarships for postgraduate (PG) students are widely available, and the specific options can vary depending on your country, field of study, and other factors.THese scholarships are mostly availed in the US region

Commonwealth Scholarships

The Commonwealth Scholarship Commission offers various scholarships for Indian students to study in Commonwealth countries, covering a wide range of fields and levels of study.

Fulbright-Nehru Scholarships

Administered by the United States-India Educational Foundation (USIEF), these scholarships fund Indian students for graduate and postgraduate studies in the United States

Chevening Scholarships

Funded by the UK government, Chevening Scholarships provide opportunities for Indian students to pursue postgraduate studies in the United Kingdom.
